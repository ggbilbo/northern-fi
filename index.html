Meet Your Consultants:

We’re ready to help you plan your future.

When you’re trying to plan for the future—while managing your busy life—you need someone in your corner. With Northern Financial Services, you do.

At Northern Financial Services, we strive to make a difference in you and your family’s financial lives. We work to uphold a fiduciary standard to provide advice, services, and products that place our clients’ needs and desires first. Our approach is to actively manage your assets and offer guidance, allowing you to be part of each step of your financial planning. Our commitment and passion drive us daily to make a difference in our client’s and their loved ones’ lives.

Northern Financial Services is dedicated to offering you tools, resources and customized solutions designed to help you pursue your financial goals and is committed to your financial wellbeing and future. We’re excited to provide you direct access to a financial services industry experienced professional to assist with your retirement and financial planning needs through our broker dealer
CUSO Financial Services, LP (CFS).

Meet the consultant who’s ready to help you plan your future.


Devin Benko
Financial Professional
CUSO Financial Services, L.P.

Devin Benko started his investment career in 2020 working at Equitable before joining CFS. He has been with CFS since 2023 and is currently a Financial Professional. Devin is excited to help individuals and families at Northern Credit Union reach their financial goals by offering detailed financial advice and helping them create personalized financial plans. He is deeply committed to his clients’ goals and well-being and strives to listen and provide assistance tailored to their unique circumstances.

When not working, Devin can be found spending time with his dog and family in his hometown of Syracuse. They enjoy spending time outdoors, hiking, camping or traveling! Devin also has a passion for community service and currently is involved in many charitable organizations and non-for-profits such as his local rotary club.

Devin believes the highest compliment he can receive from a client is a referral. Please feel free to pass along the name of a friend, colleague, or family member that you feel would benefit from a consultative discussion on their financial objectives and goals.

“I am excited to help individuals and families at Northern reach their financial goals by offering detailed financial advice and helping them create personalized financial plans.” – Devin Benko
